Welcome to ICMR 2027

The International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval (ICMR) is the premier scientific conference for multimedia retrieval worldwide. ICMR brings together researchers and practitioners to present original research, exchange ideas between academia and industry, host complementary events like workshops and panels, and facilitate networking among delegates. Join us in Singapore for cutting-edge research in multimedia information retrieval, including image, video, audio, and multimodal content analysis, search, and recommendation.
